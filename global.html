SDG-8
Problem 1:
During the pandemic 1.6 billion workers in the economy risk losing their livelihoods.

Short Term Solutions
Governments around the world, should prioritize the vaccination of these people and should work upon giving them economic aid. As an SDG warrior we must gather together, and demand the government to register them, such that the government schemes benefit them.

Long Term Solutions
Governments should work upon improving the working conditions of people. It should train people and "kill" menial jobs and should introduce new jobs with better scope of work. Being an SDG warrior we must look that how we can help in implementing this solution. We should work upon gathering funds for the same and okay a major role in widening their skill sets


Problem 2:
Slowdown in Global Economic Growth

Short Term Solutions
We as sdg warriors need to work upon developing a system that will enable people to increase their skill set hence increasing their productivity. We must also educate people regarding how taking short naps can help tremendously in productivity. We must start with neighborhoods and work our way forward to larger areas.

Long Term Solutions
The major problem is global education system and its ridgity. We must improve the way, in which people percieve the education system. We, as SDG warriors must inform this to the public and to the world such that as per country education system can be customized. While in many countries it is in practice, in majority countries it is not. This will result in our next generation being more productive and skillful.
