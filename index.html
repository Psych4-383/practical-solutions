Abstract

Finding Problems and their solutions by innovation has become human's second nature. Solutions are only decent when they are practically possible and scalable. But to find those solutions, problems need to be identified, and hence, we have divided the problems and classified them as either, local problems (where people have a major role to play for the fulfillment of the solution) and global problems, (where governments of different countries need to take action as individual person's work would be insignificant). Analyzing and understanding this would lead to solving most of our problems.

SDG-8 states that we should promote sustainable economic rise. This is an issue which has to be solved at multiple fronts, many local problems and major global problems.

SDG-11 states, we should move towards sustainable cities and communities.

In both these SDGs we can identify the fact that the concept of "sustainability" is prevalent. Hence our solutions must be in line with the expectations of the question too.

A major problem, affecting the goals of both SDGs is global collaboration which can be achieved only but commitment and determination to fulfill it. Global collaboration will also dictate the solutions proposed.

For the purpose of the solutions, we would consider full global support considering that this problem affects each and every person be it of any country. Hence keeping in mind these pointers we will be moving on to the solutions. We will also be considering an SDG warrior as a person who is part of a team of passionate #StudentChangemakers and #STEMinists on a mission to meet the Global Goals by 2030, using the power of STEM and 21CLD embedded in curriculums across the globe and any person who are working for achieving the goals present in SDGs.
