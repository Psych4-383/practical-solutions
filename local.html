Local Solutions

SDG - 8
Problem 1:
Tourism is facing unprecedented challenges.

Short Term Solutions
Governments should provide economic aid and should work upon reviving tourism industry. States like Jammu and Kashmir need to be prioritied such that governments can improve the livelihoods of people. In states like these, the whole state economy is dependent upon tourism hence improving tourism could result in improving lives of people immensely. Governmets should work upon making a board that focuses on evaluating each state on the basis of the conditions prevelant there. As SDG warrior it is important to inform people about this and bring this solution in front of the government.

Long Term Solutions
Educate people such that a tourist friendly community is formed. This will result in improving tourism drastically. As an SDG warrior we must work as a community to uplift the poor, keep our surroundings clean and promote our rich culture and tradition. While this may take time to implement it will surely be fruitful
